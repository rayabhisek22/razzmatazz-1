FAQ

Q. What is Razzmatazz?

Answer 1

It is a 2 day fest filled innovative events for school students, where they can learn as well as have fun at the same time! It is organised in the premises of National Institute of Technology, Silchar. Winners are rewarded with medals and exciting prizes. The award for Best School awaits!

Q. Students of what age group can participate in the events?

Answer 4

Students of class 6-12 can participate.

FAQ

Q. What is the drop and pick up procedure for students?

Answer 5

The students must come at the time of registration on both the days.
The students can leave the premises after the closing time. In order to leave before the closing time, the students can only leave after the organisers have talked to their parents.

FAQ

Q. What are the essentials that a student requires to have for participating in Razzmatazz?

Answer 6

The student must come in his/ her full school uniform, along with ID card/ school diary.
ID card/School diary must contain the students photograph.
ID card/ School diary must contain the session for which the ID card/School diary is issued.

FAQ

Q. Are Junior Colleges allowed to participate in Razzmataazz?

Answer 7

No, Students from Junior Colleges are not allowed to participate in Razzmataazz.
